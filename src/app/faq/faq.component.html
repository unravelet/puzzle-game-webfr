How do I play the game?
To play the game just sign up or if you are already signed up, log in.
How does the game work?
Your goal is to complete the picture, so you have to click on the puzzle parts and switch them with other parts until the picture is completed.
Can I delete my account?
No, as of right now, you can not delete your account, you can only logout and never log in again.
How do I sign up?
Go to our sign up page and fill out the form, then click on sign up and you can log in.
Where can I see my highscore?
To see your highscore just go to your profile page and click on "see all highscores".
